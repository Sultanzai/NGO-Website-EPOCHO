About

Endeavour for Provision of Opportunities and Cooperation to Humanity Organization (EPOCHO) is an Afghan non-profit, non-governmental and non-political organization founded in 2020. EPOCHO envisions Afghanistan as healthy, educated and prosperous state where all Afghans enjoy equal rights without any fear and discrimination.

EPOCHO’s mission is to serve the society and pave the way for attaining its developmental goals.

More info

EPOCHO’s thematic focus areas are Health, Education, Women Empowerment, and Humanitarian Response. EPOCHO has active cooperation with NARENJ TREE FOUNDATION based in New York, United States and board members in Canada, USA, France, Germany and Sweden.


EPOCHO came into existence as a response to the lagging society and weak governance in terms of public services founded largely by a group of demobilized, free, educated and expert Afghans. Throughout these years, EPOCHO focused primarily on gathering expert and educated Afghans around the globe, building an accountable and efficient framework able to work with national and international institutions, designing and implementing of EPOCHO’s fully funded self-projects.
